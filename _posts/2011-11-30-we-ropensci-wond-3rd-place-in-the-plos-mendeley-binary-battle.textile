title: We (rOpenSci) wond 3rd place in the PLoS-Mendeley Binary Battle!
---

We entered two of our packages for integrating with PLoS Journals (rplos) and Mendeley (RMendeley) in the <a href="http://dev.mendeley.com/api-binary-battle" target="_blank">Mendeley-PLoS Binary Battle</a>.  Get them at GitHub (<a href="https://github.com/ropensci/rplos" target="_blank">rplos</a>; <a href="https://github.com/ropensci/RMendeley" target="_blank">RMendeley</a>).



<a href="http://www.mendeley.com/blog/design-research-tools/winners-of-the-first-binary-battle-apps-for-science-contest/" target="_blank">We won third place</a>!!  This gives us a $1,000 prize, plus a <a href="http://ardrone.parrot.com/parrot-ar-drone/uk/" target="_blank">Parrot AR Drone helicopter</a>.
